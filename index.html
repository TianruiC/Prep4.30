Misunderstanding and Correction

My cause:

Average salary for undergrades with different majors, college and regions

8 out of the top 10 highest starting median salary college are engineering schools

From the salary data by The Wall Street Journal, 8 out of the top 10 highest starting median salary college are engineering schools. Only No.5 (Princeton University) and No.6 (Harvard University) are in the Ivy League.

People tends to think that schools from Ivy League will have higher salary after graduation than other schools. The fact is that only 2 of the Ivy League are in the top 10 highest starting median salary. Nowadays, the demand for engineering students are rapidly increaseing with fast developing society, which end up the salary for undergrades from engineering is higher than other type of the schools.
